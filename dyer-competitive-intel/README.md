# Dyer Auto Group — Competitive Intelligence Dashboard

Single-file web app for tracking competitors in the Treasure Coast FL market.

## Features

- **Market Overview** — KPI cards showing Dyer's market position, volume, ratings, share
- **Competitor Profiles** — 10 Florida dealers with SWOT analysis (editable in-browser)
- **Price Comparison Matrix** — 12 models across Chevy/Mazda/Subaru/Kia with competitor pricing
- **Market Trends** — Chart.js visualizations: sales by brand, market share, days-to-sell, Dyer vs market
- **Intel Feed** — Team log for competitive observations with impact ratings (localStorage)
- **Action Items** — Prioritized to-do list derived from intel (localStorage)

## Usage

Open `index.html` in any modern browser. No server required. Data persists in localStorage.

## Brands Tracked

Chevrolet · Mazda · Subaru · Kia (Dyer) vs Honda, and competitor dealer groups.
